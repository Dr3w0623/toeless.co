About our team

I’m a photographer based in West Hartford, Connecticut. All my life I’ve been interested in cameras, my mother gave me my first camera when I was 14 and it changed my life. For the first time I felt like I had a purpose and something I was good at, it made me want to strive to get better. Now that I’m 16 my mission with Toeless is to expand to reach my full potential.
